Our Story

20+ Years Experience

Welcome to Spices of India

Where tradition, flavor, and passion come together.

Hello, my name is Sudhi, and I am delighted to welcome you to Spices of India in Forney, TX. My culinary journey began in India, where I earned my degree from the Institute of Hotel Management and Catering Technology, Chennai.

I later trained at the iconic Taj Connemara, Chennai, and worked as a Chef at ITC Maratha, a Luxury Collection Hotel in Mumbai. These institutions are globally recognized for setting the gold standard in Indian hospitality and culinary arts.

Over the years, I have owned and operated several restaurants across the United States, specializing in authentic Indian and Indo-Chinese cuisine. These experiences have shaped my passion for bold flavors, premium ingredients, and traditional cooking techniques.

At Spices of India, every dish is prepared with care, authenticity, and attention to detail. It is my pleasure to bring the rich culinary heritage of India to the Forney community.
